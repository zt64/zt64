## My Powercord Plugins
[No Linux Frame](https://github.com/zt64/no-linux-frame) - Adds minimize, maximize and close buttons to the header bar.<br>
[DM Typing Indicator](https://github.com/zt64/DM-Typing-Indicator) - Adds an indicator to show if someone is typing in your DMs list.<br>
[Open In-App](https://github.com/zt64/open-in-app) - Adds support to open URLs in their related apps.<br>
[Mic Capture](https://github.com/zt64/mic-capture) - Adds a button to record short audio clips and send them as a message.<br>
